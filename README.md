PROBLEM STATEMENT
An internship opportunity appears in a WhatsApp group. Someone forwards it to another group, and then it arrives again by email. Now students are scrolling through hundreds of messages trying to find the application link, eligibility criteria, and deadline.
Because of this scattered information, students often miss deadlines, lose important links, or even apply through expired or fake opportunities.
How might we ensure students can track opportunities and deadlines without searching through endless message threats?

SOLUTION
Opportunity Trail is a web application that helps students organize opportunities in one place. Instead of searching through multiple WhatsApp groups, emails, or message threads, students can paste forwarded opportunity messages directly into the application.
The system uses AI-powered text extraction to analyze the message and automatically identify important information such as the company name, role, eligibility criteria, stipend, and deadline. This unstructured text is then converted into structured opportunity data.
The result is a clean and searchable platform where students can easily track internships, jobs, hackathons, scholarships, and other opportunities without missing deadlines.

HOW THE SYSTEM WORKS
A student receives an opportunity message in WhatsApp, email, or another platform.
The message is copied and pasted into the Smart Extract section of the app.
The AI analyzes the message and extracts structured information including:
Title
Company
Eligibility
Application link
Deadline
Location
